Month 2 — Progressive overload & muscle gain
Same PPL cycle continues. Week 5–8 upgrades every exercise to a harder variation — weighted bag (20 kg) for pull/leg, archer push-ups and pike push-up to handstand prep for push. See "Workouts by week" for full details.
At 62 kg and 170 cm, you need a consistent caloric surplus of roughly 400–600 kcal above maintenance to gain 3–4 kg per month. Maintenance for your stats + daily training is about 2,400–2,500 kcal. Target: 2,900–3,100 kcal/day.
